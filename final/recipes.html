Nancy, Scallion Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp of buttermilk
  • 3/4 cup of very cold butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup whole milk

Process

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients into a large bowl.
  3. Makes sure your butter is cold and cut it into small cubes.
  4. Use a pastry cutter or your hands to add the cold butter into the flour mixture.
  5. Add in the milk and egg until everything is evenly combined, your dough should be sticky.
  6. Add in some flour and knead for a few minutes. (Add more flour when needed if the dough becomes too sticky.)
  7. Sprinkle some flour onto a flat surface of your choice and roll the dough out so that it is roughly 1 inch in thickness.
  8. Using your biscuit cutter or glass, begin cutting out circles from the dough.
  9. You should get roughly 12 circles depending on the dough thickness.
  10. Place the circles onto your buttered or lightly greased skillet. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until the tops are golden brown.

Enjoy

  • Remove from the oven and let them rest for a bit.
  • Spread local honey or jam on top of the biscuit and consume.
